Our Story

Sisai Park Village is an eco-resort in the Chitwan district of Nepal. It is surrounded by lush forests and the tranquil Khageri River, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

The resort offers a variety of accommodation options, including dormitory rooms, luxury super deluxe rooms, and apartments. All of the rooms are built using locally sourced materials and designed to minimize their impact on the environment.

Jungle Safari Activities

Chitwan National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most biodiverse regions in Nepal. The park is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including tigers, rhinos, elephants, monkeys, and birds.

Our resort offers a variety of Jungle Safari activities that allow you to experience the park's rich natural beauty and wildlife in a responsible and sustainable manner. Our experienced guides will take you deep into the jungle, where you will have the chance to see some of the rarest animals in the world.
